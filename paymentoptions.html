Payment Options

Cash or Check

Dr. Hiestand has set standard fees for assessment, individual therapy, and family therapy. Contact Dr. Hiestand for more details about the cost of treatment.

Insurance

Dr. Hiestand accepts PPO insurance as an out-of-network provider and can provide a super-bill. Clients are asked to contact their insurance company ahead of time to ask about PPO benefits for mental health services, deductible remaining for the year, and percentage the insurer pays for out-of-network services. If you have questions about this process, Dr. Hiestand can help in some cases by contacting your insurance company directly.
