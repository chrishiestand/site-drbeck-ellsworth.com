Welcome

If you are here because you are interested in treatment, know that you have found hope. You do not have to continue to feel powerless. Dr. Hiestand helps clients foster their hope, connect to their motivation, and experience growth and change in their lives. Through her gentle and thoughtful approach, Dr. Hiestand guides and educates clients on how to take the power away from their anxiety, depression, eating disorder, traumatic memories or addictions. Clients leave sessions feeling calmer, grounded, and ready to make changes in their lives.

Dr. Danielle Hiestand is a licensed marriage and family therapist (MFT #48529) who specializes in the treatment of eating disorders, trauma, depression, and anxiety. She works with males, females, individuals, families, adolescents, and adults. Dr. Hiestand views treatment through a systems perspective and includes mindfulness, acceptance commitment therapy (ACT), dialectical behavioral therapy (DBT), and EMDR therapy in her practice. You can read more about Dr. Hiestand’s therapeutic approach here.
